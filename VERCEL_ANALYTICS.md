# How to View Vercel Analytics

## Step 1: Go to Vercel Dashboard

  1. Visit <https://vercel.com>
  2. Sign in with your account
  3. You'll see your list of projects

## Step 2: Select Your Project

- Click on your semayawi-menged-v2 project (or whatever name you deployed it as)

  ## Step 3: View Analytics

  Once inside your project:

  For Page Views & Visitors:

- Click the "Analytics" tab at the top
- You'll see:
  - Total visitors
  - Page views
  - Top pages
  - Referrers
  - Real-time data

  ### For Performance Metrics

- Click the "Speed Insights" tab
- You'll see:
  - Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS, TTFB)
  - Performance scores
  - Page-by-page breakdown
